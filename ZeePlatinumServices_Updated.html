Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How often should a driveway be sealed?

Most asphalt driveways benefit from sealing every 2-3 years. However, this can vary depending on weather exposure, traffic, and the quality of previous sealant. We offer free inspections to assess your driveway's condition and recommend the best schedule.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all our services. Contact us by phone, email, or WhatsApp to schedule your free consultation. We'll assess your project and provide a detailed quote.

Can you repair sinking interlock pavers?

Absolutely! We specialize in interlock repairs, including sinking and uneven pavers. We lift the affected area, re-grade and compact the base, then reinstall the pavers to create a perfectly level surface.

How long does driveway sealing take?

Most residential driveways can be sealed in 3-5 hours. The sealant typically dries within 24-48 hours depending on weather conditions. We'll provide specific timing when we assess your driveway.

What is polymeric sand and why do I need it?

Polymeric sand is a mixture of fine sand and polymers that hardens when activated with water. It fills the joints between pavers, preventing weeds, ants, and erosion while stabilizing your interlock. It's essential for maintaining beautiful, long-lasting interlocking.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Zee Platinum Services is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance. We're committed to operating professionally and protecting both our team and your property.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, e-transfer, cheque, and all major credit cards for your convenience. Payment terms will be discussed when we provide your estimate.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes! We stand behind our work with warranties on both materials and workmanship. Specific warranty terms vary by service and will be outlined in your project agreement.
